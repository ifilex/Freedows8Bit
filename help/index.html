About

What is this ?

This is an virtual game console with a graphical desktop for TvBOXs, Cell Phones and PC.

Why was this created ?

It was created to develop the first virtual video game console, capable of mining cryptocurrencies that are given to players for playing the games on it.

How to play?

Just select the game you want to play, and the system will download its content from the cloud so you can enjoy it on your computer.

How to earn money?

To earn money playing you have two ways to do it, one is by playing the SpaceWAR and SpaceFARM games, and the second is by claiming diamonds within the 8BIT-FARM faucet.

How to claim in the faucet?

To claim your free diamonds, you must click on the user icon located in the upper right corner. Then you must click on the "Claim Diamonds" button, each time you click on it, you will be given two free diamonds, this process can be executed infinitely as many times as you want.

How to claim your NFT?

To claim your nft you must click on the user icon, located at the top right of the desktop, choosing the option to claim tokens. Within the faucet, you will need to click on the "Claim yours NFT" menu. But remember, in order to claim your NFTs you must have at least 3000 diamonds, and enter a valid ERC20 address where you can receive your NFTs within the Polygon Matic network.

Types of Rewards

The rewards are granted in the following way: With 3000 diamonds, 1 NFT valued at 2 dollars will be assigned. With 6000 diamonds, 1 NFT valued at 4 dollars will be allocated. With 12,000 diamonds, 1 NFT valued at 8 dollars will be allocated. With 24,000 diamonds, 1 NFT valued at 32 dollars will be assigned. With 48,000 diamonds, 1 NFT valued at 64 dollars will be allocated. From 50,000 to 100,000 diamonds, rare and unique non-repeatable NFTs will be delivered, and from 100,000 to 1000,000 coins, 1 epic NFT capable of mining NFTs will be delivered every 15 days. All these NFTs will always be delivered to your ERC20 wallet under the POLYGON network, without any cost or commission.

Rules and limitations

You will be able to claim as many NFTs as diamonds you can trade, and once you have traded your diamonds, they will disappear as they will be burned to create NFTs. And you can claim from the faucet as many times as you want, regardless of the amount or quantity. Although remember that if you claim small values ​​of diamonds, the corresponding units of NFT will be delivered to you from the diamonds delivered.

Developers

If you are a developer you can port your application or game to obtain a monetary return within our platform on a monthly basis. To do this, you must first enter the development console, it is located in the Z:\ icon of the main menu. The SDK is made up of the BORLAND C, TURBO C, DJGPP and NASM compilers, all with graphical libraries for game and application development. Once your application works correctly within the SDK, you can export it by sending it in compressed format, if possible with a name and icon, attaching your ERC20 wallet to the email: 8bitfarm.nft@gmail.com, later we will publish your application within the store , starting to receive from the 1st to the 5th of each month a total of 30 NFT in your declared wallet.
