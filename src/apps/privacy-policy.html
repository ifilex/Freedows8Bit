Privacy Policy: Winebox (Neurostimulation App) Last Updated: March 22, 2026 At Winebox, privacy and cognitive safety are our top priorities. This application is designed under the Total Privacy (Zero Data) standard. 1. No Data Collection Winebox does not collect, request, store, or transmit any type of personal data. We do not use registration forms, emails, or names. We do not track cognitive performance or progress on external servers. All data generated through the use of the app remains encrypted locally on the user's device. 2. Use by Minors and Responsibility Although Winebox’s content is pedagogically designed for children, the application is categorized for ages 18 and up in official stores. Access to the tool by a minor must be carried out exclusively under the direct monitoring and supervision of a parent, guardian, or responsible healthcare professional. Winebox assumes no responsibility for the unsupervised use of the application by minors. 3. Third-Party Clients (Steam, GOG, Amazon, Epic) Winebox functions as an access client for external game libraries. Login and credential management occur directly on the official platforms of these providers. Winebox does not have access to passwords, banking details, or account information from these external services. 4. Permissions The application only requests the minimum technical permissions required to run local games and processes, without accessing private device information such as contacts, location, or personal files unrelated to the app. 5. Contact As a local-use application with no data collection, there are no databases for the user to manage or delete. For technical inquiries, the user may contact the developer directly.
