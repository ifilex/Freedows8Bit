Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Zen Oracle application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Zero Personal Data Collection

Zen Oracle does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users. We do not require account creation, nor do we request names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

2. Health and Wellness Data Declaration (Exhaustive)

In compliance with Google Play’s Health and User Data policies, we explicitly declare:

  • No Access to Health Data: The App DOES NOT access, collect, or use any data from health-related APIs (such as Health Connect, Google Fit, or Apple Health).
  • No Biometric Sensors: The App DOES NOT access or use heart rate sensors, pulse measurements, sleep tracking, or any other biometric hardware.
  • Non-Medical Nature: Zen Oracle is strictly a philosophical and entertainment tool based on I Ching and Zen principles. It is not a medical device, nor does it provide medical diagnosis, clinical treatment, or health monitoring.
  • Breathing Features: Any visual or auditory breathing guides within the app are for atmospheric and meditative purposes only. No physiological data is measured, recorded, or analyzed.

3. Data Usage and Local Storage

The App utilizes local storage on your device (localStorage) solely to save interface preferences and local reading history.

  • Privacy: This information never leaves your device. We do not have servers that store your app usage or philosophical inquiries.
  • Third-Party Access: We do not share, sell, or transmit any user-generated content or local data to third parties.

4. Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain non-sensitive device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. This process is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

We only request Internet Access to load advertisements and basic app functionality. We DO NOT request access to:

  • Contacts or Calendar
  • Microphone or Camera
  • Files, Photos, or Private Storage

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not store data in the cloud, you have 100% control over your data. You can permanently delete all local history and settings at any time by clearing the application cache in your Android Settings or by uninstalling the App.
