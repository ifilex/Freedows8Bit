Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Zen Oracle application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Zen Oracle does not collect, store on external servers, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users, such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Health and Wellness Data

The App includes wellness features, specifically guided breathing techniques for relaxation and cognitive stimulation purposes.

  • Access: The App does NOT access biometric sensors, camera for pulse measurement, or health APIs (such as Google Fit).
  • Usage: These features are purely visual and auditory. Users are not required to input any medical or health-related data.
  • Storage: No records regarding breathing frequency, heart rate, or the user's health status are recorded or stored.

3. Local Storage and Data Usage

The App uses your device's local storage (localStorage) solely to save interface preferences and reading history. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since Zen Oracle does not store data in the cloud, we do not maintain permanent records of personal information.

  • How to delete your data: The user has full control. You can delete all usage data and history at any time by clearing the application cache in your Android settings or by uninstalling the application.
  • Upon uninstallation, all local information is permanently and immediately removed from the device.

5. Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device data to serve relevant ads, including Android advertising IDs and approximate IP addresses. This process is independent and governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. App Permissions

We request only Internet Access permissions for ad loading and basic functionality. We do not request access to contacts, microphone, camera, or private files.

7. Children's Privacy

Zen Oracle is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including minors.
