Privacy Policy for Lizard PRO

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lizard PRO handles information. Our priority is the absolute privacy of the independent healthcare professional (Physician, Psychologist, or Therapist) and their clinical data. This application is designed as a secure, personal digital environment for private practice and forensic expertise.

CRITICAL PRIVACY DISCLOSURE: Lizard PRO is a strictly LOCAL application. All clinical data, patient records, and session logs are stored exclusively on the user's device and are NEVER uploaded to the internet or external servers.

1. Data Storage and Sovereignty

Lizard PRO adheres to a "Local-First" architecture to ensure professional secrecy:

  • No Cloud Storage: The application does not utilize cloud databases or remote syncing services.
  • Offline Architecture: The app functions entirely without an internet connection, ensuring that sensitive data remains isolated from web-based threats.
  • User Control: You own and control 100% of the data. No information is transmitted to the developer or any third party.

2. Data Collection Overview

Data Category Collection Status Storage Location
Professional Identifiers Not Collected Local Device Only
Patient Medical Records Not Collected Local Device Only
Forensic Reports Not Collected Local Device Only
Usage Analytics Not Collected N/A

3. Security Responsibilities

Since all data resides on your local hardware, the security of the information is the responsibility of the professional. We strongly recommend:

  • Enabling full-disk encryption on your mobile device.
  • Using biometric locks or strong alphanumeric passwords.
  • Performing local backups according to your professional ethics and local regulations.

4. Inter-Device Connectivity (Bluetooth)

Lizard PRO allows for local data sharing between devices via Bluetooth. This process is encrypted and occurs directly between hardware units without passing through any external servers or the internet.

5. Clinical & Forensic Standards

By maintaining a zero-server footprint, Lizard PRO facilitates the professional's compliance with international data protection laws (such as HIPAA, GDPR, or local forensic protocols) by preventing unauthorized data transfers.

6. Contact

For technical inquiries regarding the local architecture of Lizard PRO, please contact the developer through the official Google Play Store contact information.
