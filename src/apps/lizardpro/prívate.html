Privacy Policy - Lizard PRO

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lizard PRO collects, uses, and protects user information. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your professional and clinical data.

1. Data Collection and Local Storage

Lizard PRO is designed as a local management tool. All clinical records, patient information, and professional data entered into the application are stored exclusively on the user's device. The application does not automatically upload, sync, or transmit this sensitive data to external servers or cloud databases.

2. Use of Information

The information is used solely to provide the application's core functionality, including medical history management, clinical organization, and professional consultation. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention and Storage Duration

Data is retained in the device's local storage for as long as the application is installed and the user maintains their profile. Because the developer does not host your data on external servers, the duration of data storage is entirely under the user's control.

4. Data Deletion and Right to be Forgotten

In compliance with Google Play policies regarding user data, Lizard PRO provides clear mechanisms for data deletion:

  • Manual Deletion: Users can delete individual records, patient files, or specific entries at any time through the application's user interface.
  • Full Data Erasure: To delete all data permanently, the user can uninstall the application or use the "Clear Data" option in the Android System Settings. This action is irreversible and removes all locally stored databases.
  • Deletion Requests: Since data is stored locally, the developer cannot remotely delete your information. However, if you require assistance on how to ensure all traces of data are removed from your device, you may contact us at ifilex@gmail.com.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the information stored on your device. Users are encouraged to use device-level security features (such as screen locks and encryption) to further protect their professional data.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Significant changes will be notified through the application or updated on our website.
