Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WineBOX Tool ("the Application", "we", "us") handles information. We are committed to protecting the privacy of healthcare and neurodevelopmental professionals who use our services.

Key Declaration: WineBOX Tool is designed as a local management utility. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from our users or their patients to external servers.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Unlike traditional management software, WineBOX Tool operates under a Zero-Data Collection policy:

  • Patient Data: All clinical records, neurodevelopmental screenings, and patient information are stored exclusively in the device's local storage or the user's private environment. We have no access to this data.
  • User Information: We do not require account registration on our own servers. Your identity is managed through your Google Play account.

2. Payments and Billing (Google Play)

Our application uses Google Play Billing for subscriptions (Monthly, Yearly) and Lifetime access.

  • We do not process or store credit card numbers or financial details.
  • All transaction data is handled securely by Google. We only receive a confirmation "token" to unlock professional features.

3. GDPR Compliance (European Union)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we respect the following principles:

  • Data Minimization: We do not ask for more information than is strictly necessary for the app to function.
  • Integrity and Confidentiality: Data remains under the professional's control at all times.
  • Right to Erasure: Since data is local, users can delete all information simply by clearing the application's data or uninstalling it.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. No data from your clinical management is shared with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Professional Use Disclaimer

WineBOX Tool is a management and support tool for professionals. It is not an automated diagnostic device. Professionals are responsible for the clinical interpretation of the data managed within the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date".

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the official support channels provided in the Google Play Store listing.
