Privacy Policy: ZenAxis

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

At ZenAxis, we believe that mental well-being requires a space free of data tracking. This application is built with a Privacy-First (Zero Data Collection) philosophy.

1. No Personal Data Collection

ZenAxis does not collect, request, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account registration, names, or email addresses. Your cognitive performance or neurostimulation results are not tracked on external servers.

2. Local Storage and Data Persistence

To provide a consistent experience, the app uses LocalStorage to save your progress and your "Jardín del Alma" history directly on your device.

  • This data remains exclusively on your device.
  • It is never shared with the developer or third parties.
  • Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will permanently delete this local progress.

3. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use pseudonymous identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to show relevant ads. These identifiers do not allow ZenAxis to access your personal identity.

4. Use by Minors

While the content is designed for mindfulness and stimulation, the application is categorized for ages 18 and up in official stores. Use by minors must be supervised by a parent, guardian, or healthcare professional.

5. Permissions

We only request technical permissions required for the app's operation (Internet access for AdMob and local storage). We do not access your contacts, location, or private files.
